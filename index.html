What We Do

Students Foundry harnesses the power of artificial intelligence and automation to transform raw educational data into meaningful insights. Our platform analyzes student performance patterns, engagement metrics, and learning behaviors to provide schools with actionable intelligence.

We seamlessly integrate with existing school management systems, learning platforms, and assessment tools, requiring minimal technical setup while delivering maximum value to educators, administrators, and parents alike.

Core Features

Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to provide deep insights into student performance and engagement.

5-Metric Performance Dashboard

Track attendance, grades, participation, assignment completion, and growth metrics in one centralized, easy-to-interpret dashboard.

Real-Time Engagement Tracking

Monitor student engagement across digital platforms, classroom activities, and learning resources with live updates and historical trends.

AI-Powered Insights & Alerts

Receive proactive notifications about performance changes, potential issues, and opportunities for intervention before they become problems.

Teacher–Parent Communication

Facilitate data-driven conversations between educators and families with shareable reports, automated updates, and collaborative goal-setting tools.

Custom Reports

Generate tailored reports for different stakeholders, from district-level analytics to individual student progress summaries with just a few clicks.

Seamless Integration

Connect with over 50 popular educational platforms and student information systems with our secure API and pre-built connectors.
